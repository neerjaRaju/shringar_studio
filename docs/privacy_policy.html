Privacy Policy — Shringar Studio

Last updated: 20 July 2026 · Developer: Zrix · Contact: satish@zrix.com

This Privacy Policy explains how the Shringar Studio Android application ("the App") handles information. By using the App you agree to this policy. We designed the App to be privacy-friendly: it requires no account, no sign-in, and stores your personal activity only on your device.

1. Information the App Does Not Collect

  • We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or any account.
  • We do not collect or upload your photos, contacts, or files.
  • Your favorites, downloads, collections, recently-viewed items and settings are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to us.

2. Information Collected by Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App is free and supported by ads served through Google AdMob. To show ads, Google and its partners may collect and process:

  • Device advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID / AD_ID).
  • Approximate location derived from IP address, device type and OS version.
  • Ad interaction and app-usage signals used to serve and measure ads.

This processing is performed by Google, not by us. Learn more in Google's Privacy & Terms and the AdMob data disclosure. For users in the EEA, UK and Switzerland, we present a consent choice (Google User Messaging Platform) before serving personalised ads. You can change your choice anytime in the App under Settings → Ad privacy choices.

3. Voice Search

If you use the optional voice-search feature, the App requests microphone access and uses your device's speech-recognition service to convert speech to text for searching designs. Audio is processed to produce the search text and is not stored or shared by us. You can use the App fully without granting the microphone permission.

4. Network Requests

The App downloads design images and periodic database updates from public GitHub-hosted sources and content-delivery networks (jsDelivr, Statically, GitHub). These requests include standard technical information (such as your IP address) required to deliver the files. No personal identifiers are sent by us in these requests.

5. Permissions Used

  • Internet / Network state — to download images and updates and to show ads.
  • Microphone — only for optional voice search.
  • Set wallpaper — only when you choose to set a design as wallpaper.
  • Advertising ID — used by AdMob as described above.

6. Children

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ads are configured with a family-safe maximum content rating.

7. Your Choices & Rights

  • Reset or limit ad personalisation in your device's Settings → Google → Ads.
  • Change ad-consent (EEA/UK) via Settings → Ad privacy choices in the App.
  • Clear all locally-stored data by clearing the App's storage or uninstalling it.
  • Depending on your region (e.g. GDPR, CCPA), you may have rights to access or delete data held by Google; see Google's policy linked above.

8. Data Security & Retention

All network traffic uses HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit. Because we do not operate servers or accounts, we do not retain personal data about you. On-device data persists until you delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date on this page.

10. Contact
